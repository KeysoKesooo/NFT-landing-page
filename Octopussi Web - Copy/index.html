WELCOME

The Whale Society, a collection of sophisticated and stylish majestic whales dressed in elegant attire.
Our society is a celebration of the regal beauty and grace of these magnificent creatures, as well as a nod to the whales on Cardano.

FAMILIAS

The Fluke-Fins, the intellectuals of The Whale Society. They are always seeking new knowledge and pushing the boundaries of what is possible to accumulate, both in the ocean and on land.


The Blubberbottoms, the most eccentric of the families. They are always throwing lavish parties and indulging in the finest foods and wines, much to the amusement of the other families.


The McWhales are the philosophers of the society. They are renowned for their wisdom and deep understanding of the world around them.


The Seaworthys are the newest and most ambitious family. They are determined to make their mark on The Whale Society and climb the ranks to become the most powerful family of them all.


The Whaleys are the oldest and most prestigious family, tracing their lineage back to the very beginning of The Whale Society. They are known for their wealth, influence, and impeccable manners.
